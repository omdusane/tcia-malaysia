Why Public Cloud

Leverage scalable, reliable, and cost-effective IT resources offered by public cloud providers

Welcome to Why Public Cloud:

Unleashing the Power of Scalable, Reliable, and Cost-Effective IT Resources

Unlock the potential of your business by leveraging the advantages offered by public cloud providers.

Here's why you should consider the public cloud for your IT infrastructure:

Scalability:

  • Maintain Upscale as Per Requirements: Save costs on unused data by scaling resources in line with business needs.
  • Future-Proof IT Infrastructure: Easily plan for future resource allocation with scalable benefits.
  • Concentration on Core Business Requirements: Reduce workload, allowing you to focus on core business requirements.
  • Cloud-Based Tools for Assessment and Migration: Leverage additional cloud-based tools for effective assessment and migration.

Cost-Effective:

  • Reduced Costs: A popular strategy with 88% of companies saving money when running services in the cloud.
  • Eliminate Ongoing Maintenance and Support Costs: Local servers incur ongoing costs; cloud services eliminate these.
  • No Need for On-Site Staff: Reduce expenses by eliminating the need for on-site staff to manage systems.
  • Avoid Expensive Systems and Equipment Purchases: Cloud eliminates the need for purchasing expensive systems and equipment.

Remote Collaboration:

  • Access Anywhere: Cloud allows remote access to documents and databases, fostering effective collaboration.
  • Efficient Remote Work: Enables workers to collaborate remotely, reducing inefficiencies associated with file sharing.

Better Work-Life Balance:

  • Flexible Working Hours and Locations: Cloud facilitates a better work-life balance, enabling flexible work hours and remote work.
  • Platform and Device Independence: Services are independent of platforms and devices, working seamlessly on mobiles, tablets, and laptops.

Integration:

  • Seamless Integration: Integrate with other systems in a seamless and cost-effective manner within the cloud environment.

Better Storage:

  • High-Security Data Storage: Cloud providers offer high-security data storage at a minimum cost.
  • Scalable Storage: Easily expand or shrink storage based on business requirements.

Disaster Recovery:

  • Efficient Data Recovery: Cloud-based backup and recovery solutions make data recovery efficient.
  • Ideal for Smaller Companies: Cloud-based solutions are ideal for smaller companies, saving time and investment.

Automation of Tasks:

  • Automatic Updating and API Integration: Cloud environment allows easy automation of repetitive tasks.

Flexibility:

  • Fluctuating Demands: Easily handle fluctuating bandwidth demands and storage needs with cloud storage.
  • Scalability: Scale up cloud capacity as needed for testing, deploying applications, and installing/removing applications easily.

Mobility:

  • Secure Connection Access: Enables secure connection access to cloud data even in uncontrolled situations.
  • PaaS Cloud Services: Commonly available, offering IP whitelisting features regardless of the device.

Security:

  • Enhanced Security Measures: Cloud environments lock away security issues like hacking, data theft, and unauthorized access.

Faster Setup:

  • Quick and Easy Setup: Set up data and applications in the cloud within a matter of hours.

Less Maintenance:

  • Managed by Cloud Provider: No maintenance required as the cloud provider manages hardware, software, and networks.

Agility:

  • Quicker Decision Making: Cloud services facilitate quicker decision-making, reducing effort spent on infrastructure.

Zero Risk Failure:

  • 99% Uptime: Cloud providers offer 99% uptime, ensuring smooth and continuous performance.

Maximum Uptime:

  • Choose Providers with Maximum Uptime: Ideal for applications requiring web-based, customer-facing, and multi-user access.

Competitiveness:

  • Enterprise-Class Technology: Cost-effective, pay-as-you-go services make your business globally available, reducing maintenance costs.

Environment-Friendly:

  • Sustainable Solutions: Adopt sustainable solutions with less environmental impact, reducing costs and benefiting small businesses.

Choose the public cloud to experience scalable, reliable, and cost-effective IT resources, unlocking the full potential of your business in the digital era.
