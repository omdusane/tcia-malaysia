AWS Back & DR as a Service

AWS offers Backup as a Service (BaaS) and Disaster Recovery as a Service (DRaaS), allowing businesses to efficiently protect and recover their data in the AWS cloud environment

Welcome to AWS Back & DR as a Service:

Welcome to AWS Back & DR as a Service at TCiA Global, where we leverage AWS's Backup as a Service (BaaS) and Disaster Recovery as a Service (DRaaS) to empower businesses with efficient data protection and recovery within the AWS cloud environment.

Why Choose TCiA Global for AWS Backup & DR as a Service?

No Lost Downtime:

Ensure uninterrupted operations and eliminate data loss with our AWS Backup & DR solution. Benefit from 1-click recovery in seconds, achieving near-zero Recovery Time Objective (RTO) for complete business continuity.

Set It and Forget It:

Save time and streamline operations with our fully automated and flexible backup policies. With scheduled backups for your AWS workloads, our solution requires minimal maintenance, allowing you to set it up and let it run.

Tangible Savings:

Avoid surprises in your bill by automatically managing old snapshots, resulting in a 60% reduction in long-term retention costs. Implement Resource Control to turn off non-critical instances, providing tangible savings for your organization.

Peace of Mind:

Meet compliance requirements by retaining critical data in a low-cost storage tier within the AWS environment. Rest easy, as our solution, provided by N2WS, is non-proprietary, ensuring an easy "decoupling" story for seamless integration.

Extremely Easy:

Experience a hassle-free setup with our user-friendly interface and 1-click CloudFormation. Automate the backup and recovery of business-critical data within minutes, without leaving your console, allowing you to focus on strategic initiatives.

Fills in the Gaps of AWS:

As the first AWS cloud-native backup & DR tool, our solution seamlessly fills in the gaps in the AWS model. Benefit from flexible policies, automation, application-consistent backups, a restful API, and the ability to manage thousands of instances effortlessly.

Ready to enhance your data protection and disaster recovery capabilities in the AWS cloud? Contact us today for a consultation and let's build a robust strategy tailored to your business needs.
